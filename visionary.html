Our Visionary

At the helm of House of Brands Fashion LLP is Rajan Mehta

An accomplished engineer with a master's degree from IIT Calcutta. With over 22 years of experience in the fashion industry, Rajan has collaborated with global brands such as Lee Cooper, Puma, Timex, and Reebok. His extensive career spans product development, manufacturing, and retail operations.

Engineering Excellence in Fashion

Rajan's journey began with a passion for precision engineering, which seamlessly transformed into the foundation for exquisite and fashion-conscious designs. His unique blend of technical expertise and global fashion trends has allowed him to bridge the gap between classic craftsmanship and contemporary aesthetics.

Throughout his career, Rajan has led numerous successful launches and collaborations, driving growth for international brands and establishing them in the market. His vision to integrate the strength, vision, and ability to identify emerging trends have consistently positioned the brands he works with at the forefront of fashion and technology.

"Under the guidance of House of Brands, Rajan's leadership extends beyond product development. He fosters a culture of creativity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality and attention to detail, ensuring that every product reflects not just style but also superior craftsmanship and durability."

- Industry Recognition
