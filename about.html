About Us

Welcome to House of Brands Fashion LLP

Who We Are

House of Brands Fashion LLP is a premier design and manufacturing company specializing in crafting high-quality watches, sunglasses, leather accessories, footwear, apparel, and more for men, women, and kids. With over two decades of expertise, we combine timeless craftsmanship with modern trends to create fashion essentials that resonate with contemporary consumers.

Our Expertise

We excel in developing exclusive collections that reflect evolving fashion sensibilities, ensuring every product embodies superior quality, style, and durability. From sophisticated men's and women's watches to chic sunglasses, belts, wallets, jewelry, and clutches, our diverse portfolio caters to a wide array of fashion needs.

Our Partners

We are proud to collaborate with some of the largest e-commerce giants and retail leaders, including Myntra, Flipkart, Noon, Ajio, FirstCry, Hopscotch, Amazon, and Shoppers Stop. As a trusted OEM partner, we design and manufacture exclusive private label products, ensuring alignment with each brand's identity and target audience.

Private Label Collaborations

House of Brands plays a key role in shaping the product lines of renowned private label brands like Dressberry, Roadster, Mast & Harbour, HRX, Invictus, Wrogn, Killer, and Provogue. Our designs span across categories such as watches, sunglasses, leather goods, and kids' accessories, bringing fashion-forward products to consumers across multiple platforms.

Global Reach

With partnerships extending to the UAE and other international markets through platforms like Noon.com, our designs reach customers worldwide, showcasing the excellence of Indian craftsmanship on a global stage.

Why Choose House of Brands

At House of Brands, we prioritize innovation, quality, and attention to detail. Our products are not just accessories; they are statements of style, reflecting the latest trends while maintaining timeless appeal.

Whether you're looking for premium leather wallets, elegant clutches, trendy sunglasses, or bespoke kids' watches, House of Brands delivers fashion that speaks to every generation.

Join Us in Crafting Fashion

Explore our collections and experience the fusion of design, craftsmanship, and quality that defines House of Brands Fashion LLP. Together, let's shape the future of fashion.
